I spent the month of July at the [Institute for lifecourse Development (ILD)](https://www.gre.ac.uk/institute-lifecourse-development) at the [University of Greenwich](https://www.gre.ac.uk/), of which I am a visiting fellow.  A very productive visit, during which my colleague Kafui and I made great progresses in our analyses for the ExPoSe project.  
<br/>  
During the visit I met with many other colleagues at the ILD and at the [University College London (UCL)](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/population-health-sciences/), and I also given an invited [talk](/talk/ild2022/) for a ILD public Lecture.
