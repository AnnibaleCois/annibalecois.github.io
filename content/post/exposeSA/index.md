The [Division of Health Systems and Public Health](https://www.sun.ac.za/english/faculty/healthsciences/global-health/health-systems-public-health) at Stellenbosch University is organising a research dissemination and knowledge sharing event to launch the finding of the project, **Explaining Population Trends in Cardiovascular Risk: A Comparative Analysis of Health Transitions in South Africa and England** (**ExPoSE**). 

The project uses nationally-representative population surveys to examine and explain trends in cardiovascular risk in South Africa and England from 1998 to 2017.  The project is also producing a harmonised dataset, code and a data dashboard which will be made publicly available for other researchers and practitioners to use, and these will be showcased during the event.

The event will be held on the **11th of August 2023** at the [Stellenbosch Institute for Advanced Study (Stiαs)](https://stias.ac.za/),Wallenberg Research Centre, 10 Marais Road, Stellenbosch, South Africa, from 9 AM to 4 PM, and the possibility of remote participation (via Microsoft Teams) is also be provided.
