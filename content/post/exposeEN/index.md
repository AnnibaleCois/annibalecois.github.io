On the **4th of September 2023** Prof. Kafui Adjaye-Gbewonyo from Greenwich University and I have coordinated a research dissemination and knowledge sharing event to launch the finding of the project, **Explaining Population Trends in Cardiovascular Risk: A Comparative Analysis of Health Transitions in South Africa and England** (**ExPoSE**) in England.

The event took place at the [University of Greenwich](https://stias.ac.za/),Queen Anne Court, Park Row London SE10 9NW, UK, from 9 AM to 4 PM, and the possibility of remote participation (via Microsoft Teams) was also be provided.

Presenter at the event included:

-   **Prof. Claire Monk, Institute for Lifecourse Development, University of Greenwich**
-   **Dr Kafui Adjaye-Gbewonyo, Institute for Lifecourse Development, University of Greenwich**
-   **Dr Annibale Cois, Department of Global Health, Stellenbosch University**
-   **Dr Charlotte Jeavons, Scholl of Human Sciences, University of Greenwich**
-   **Prof. Jennifer Mindell, Health and Social Surveys Research Group, University College London**
-   **Dr Shaun Sholes, Health and Social Surveys Research Group, University College London**
-   **Prof. Ed Frottel, Centre for Global NCDs, University College London***
-   **Dr Stuart Rison, Wolfson Institute of Population Health, Queen Mary University**
-   **Dr Perviz Asaria, FAculty of Medicine, Imperial College London**
-   **Ms Mari Toomse-Smith, National centre for Social Research**
