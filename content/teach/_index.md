## Supervision of students' projects

* **[Patterns of Obesity among Pregnant and Postpartum Women in South Africa (PhD Public Health)]()**  
Improve  understanding of the role of adiposity during pregnancy and the postpartum. 

* **[Enhancing T2DM patient-provider communication through AI-powered technologies (PhD Public Health)]()**  
Exploring how AI-driven technologies can be harnessed to enhance patient-provider communication for diagnosis and treatment on non-communicable diseases. 

* **[Impact of LEAP officer deployment on homicide trends in priority police precincts of the Cape Metro? An interrupted time series analysis (MMed)]()**
